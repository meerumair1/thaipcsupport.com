WHAT YOU RECEIVE




  • A full suite of the latest Microsoft Office productivity software, accessible from anywhere on any device. Familiar tools including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook using standard file formats like DOC, XLS, and PPT.
  • Microsoft Exchange Server – the global standard for all your email and calendar needs, hosted in the Microsoft cloud with the best in anti-virus and anti-spam technology.
  • Microsoft Teams – Your one-stop hub for internal communications. Private or group instant messaging, audio chat, video conferencing with screen sharing, file-sharing and virtual whiteboards, notebooks and wikis on all your devices.
  • Microsoft OneDrive for Business – 1TB of cloud storage per user with enterprise-grade security, file or folder sharing and collaboration.
  • Microsoft OneNote – Your very own digital notebook. Take notes with text, images, audio, video, links and files. Share with others inside and outside your organization. Supports handwriting with finger or stylus.
  • Microsoft SharePoint – A powerful intranet that lets you work on documents together, monitor a project’s progress from anywhere, and host in-house information portals.
  • Microsoft Planner – Project management tools with tasks synced to Outlook and To Do.
  • Microsoft To Do – Personal to do lists in the cloud on your PC and phone, including all your tasks from Outlook and Planner.
  • Office Delve – Search for keywords across all apps and files on your Office 365, with recently accessed items for all users.
  • Microsoft Flow – Automation for useful tasks. Chain apps together to automate common, repetitive workflows and gather useful data. No coding required.
  • Yammer – A private social network for your business with all the features you're used to - timelines, likes, follows, groups... Plus enterprise-level admin tools.
  • Power BI – Business Intelligence with powerful data visualization tools and natural language queries.
  • Office 365 online can be used on almost any platform that has a browser and an internet connection, including touchscreen devices. Desktop apps are available for Windows and macOS. Native apps are also available for iOS, iPadOS, and Android.
  • On plans that include apps, each user can install Office on up to 5 PCs or Macs + 5 phones + 5 tablets.
